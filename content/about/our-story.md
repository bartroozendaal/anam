# Our Story

Anam is a project dedicated to creating simple, elegant web experiences. We believe that content should be easy to manage and beautiful to present.

## Our Mission

To provide a framework that makes it easy for anyone to build a content-driven website without worrying about complex setups or technical barriers.

## Our Values

- **Simplicity**: Keep things simple and straightforward
- **Elegance**: Create beautiful, clean designs
- **Accessibility**: Make the web available to everyone
- **Community**: Foster collaboration and shared learning
